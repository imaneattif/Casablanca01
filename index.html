Welcome to Casablanca

Explore the rich history, vibrant culture, and exciting activities of this beautiful city.

Featured Attractions in Casablanca

Hassan II Mosque

One of the largest mosques in the world, featuring stunning architecture and an oceanfront setting.

The Corniche

A scenic waterfront promenade, perfect for leisurely walks, dining, and enjoying the ocean views.

Old Medina

Explore the traditional side of Casablanca in the Old Medina, a labyrinth of narrow streets and historic buildings.

Morocco Mall

One of the largest shopping centers in Africa, offering a wide range of shops, entertainment, and dining options.

Mahkama du Pacha

A historic building known for its stunning Moorish architecture, intricate wood carvings, and beautiful tile work.
